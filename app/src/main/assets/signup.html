Fitmaker Activity Tracker Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy Applies to our Fitmaker Activity Tracker personal fitness and tracker products. We aim to continue to improve our products to help you lead a healthier life, therefore this policy may change over time, but any future changes will not affect data that was collected under a previous version of this policy. If any modifications substantially change your rights, we will send an email summarizing the changes to the address associated with your Fitmaker Activity Tracker account.

And remember, we're here to help. If something in this policy does not make sense or if you have any questions, please contact us.

Data collection and processing

Activate a Device: When activating a Fitmaker Activity Tracker Device, you will be asked to download the Fitmaker Activity Tracker App from Google Play or the Apple App Store and enter information about yourself, such as height, weight and gender. We will use this information to personalize your fitness stats like calories burned and distance traveled.

Information you provide. If you create an account, you provide us your email and a password. Your email address will be your Fitmaker Activity Tracker account user name, which you will use to log-into your account. We do not display your email address to other users. Instead, others will see the name or nickname you enter in your profile settings. You may also provide other information such as your gender, height and weight in order to use all of the App's features.

You can also create your Fitmaker Activity Tracker account using a Facebook account. We will ask permission to access basic information from that account, such as your name, profile picture, and friend list. You can stop sharing this information with us at any time by removing all the data.

When You Add Friends

Through our Fitmaker Activity Tracker App, you can add friends who are already Fitmaker Activity Tracker users or invite friends who have not yet joined. You can add friends using their email addresses, by accessing Facebook accounts or from your contact list on your phone. When you share your friends' contact information with us to add them as a friend, we will only use it for this purpose. We will not store your phone's contact list, and it is deleted immediately after it is used for this purpose.

Communications with us. If you want to have any communication with us, we collect the information and content you provide to us, including personal information such as your name, email or other contact information. You can provide anonymous feedback by using an email account that does not reveal your identity. We guarantee complete discretion with your person information.

How we use Data

This App is supposed to provide you with an easy way to track your movements during your daily life. We upload your information from your device over a secured connection to our servers in order to analyze the data and identify your activities. Your results are stored on our secure servers and are sent back to your account over a secured connection so that you can view and interact with them.

 

Your data also helps us to continue to improve our App; we use your data to develop aggregated analyses and reports that help us improve our algorithms, measure and understand how our services are used. It also helps us to develop new products, services and features such as by recognizing popular activities. We also use your information to communicate with you, such as by sending you notifications and service-related messages, or by responding to your requests and questions.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may collect information about your use of our services through the use of cookies, pixels or similar technologies. For example, we may use this information to provide or improve our services, for measurement and analytics to better understand how our services function and are used, or for internal operations. Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored on your mobile phone, computer, or other device. Pixels, often used in connection with cookies, are small instances of code placed in webpages that do things like allow another server to measure how frequently webpages are viewed and used.

Third parties may also use cookies to collect information about your activities on the App. Your App may offer you control over the cookies or other technologies when you use the services. For example, you may be able to set your set to disable, clear, reset or block the use of cookies or similar technologies.

However, we cannot guarantee the security of your data, which may be compromised by unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors.

Data retention and deletion

We may retain information regarding you and your use of the services, including personal information, for as long as needed to provide you with the services and the uses described in this Privacy Policy. This often means that we will keep information for the duration of your account.
